Project

The graphic project applied to Brastemp Retro’s fridge propose a revival of icons of the brand's history – such as the Eskimo character applied to the panel and the Brastemp badge of the 50s - with a contemporary reinterpretation. All the graphic elements work with colours, fonts and pictograms inspired by the universe of appliances from that time.
