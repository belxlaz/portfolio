Brastemp Ative! A global washer & dryer machine project

My role
Graphic Design, CFM, UI/UX Design
Team
Whirlpool - Industrial Design (Global)
Awards
IF Design

Briefing

This product was a Whirlpool Global project which brought together the Brazilian, Asian and European Design teams. The main challenge was to combine the consumers needs from different cultures in a single product platform.

Solution

Design to be simple and elegant, with outstanding features, developed through constant assessments with consumers, as usability tests, perceived quality and heuristic evaluations. Based in each country differences the CFM, graphic design and the UI behaviour were adapted.

{{ img('projects-ares-interface.jpg', 'Interface') }}

Interface

A central white backlit knob with surrounding luminesce was embedded to an elegant and slim user interface. The emotinal experience is enhanced by fading lights that follows the status of the washing and drying cycles. The dead-front display with touch sensors brings cutting-edge technologies to traditional white goods. The UI was projected to help even inexperienced users in choosing the better settings for their clothes.

{{ img('projects-ares-colours.jpg', 'Colours') }}

Colours, Finishes and Materials

In terms of colors, Brastemp Ative! offers 3 CMF options which easily blend into any home environment: Global White, Satin Chrome and All Black.
